# Benchmark Validity Analysis

## Valid Comparison

Scholarium can be compared to Academia.edu for researcher profile, publication, migration, readership, alert, and discovery workflows.

Scholarium can be compared to Google Scholar only for indexing readiness, not as a social or auth integration.

## Partial Comparison

Scholarium is pre-alpha and does not have the mature network effect, citation graph, or reader analytics of Academia.edu. Current comparisons should focus on product architecture and workflow coverage.

## Misleading Claims To Avoid

- Do not claim Google Scholar is connected until pages are crawlable and metadata-compliant.
- Do not claim Academia.edu parity until profile, analytics, alerts, search alerts, citation graph, and bulk import/export are implemented.
- Do not treat Google login as Drive, YouTube, or Antigravity authorization.

## Accepted Benchmark Claims

- OpenAI/ChatGPT entry is currently the cleanest WebAuth path in Scholarium.
- Google OAuth is implemented but needs a provider capability layer.
- Google Scholar natural connection should be implemented as public page metadata, robots, sitemap, identifiers, and stable artifact URLs.
- Scholarium's planned differentiation is provenance, transparent discovery, no pay-to-rank, and Education-suite tooling.

# Roadmap

## Pre-alpha

- Stabilize the Omeka public theme.
- Keep all public write paths locked.
- Publish owner/Codex-created foundation records.
- Build public search, collections, tags, and publication detail views.
- Document the metadata model for white papers, book submissions, and research notes.

## Alpha Gate

Alpha starts only when SecuredMe can safely support a real free alternative to paywalled academic networks:

- moderation workflow exists;
- submission review workflow exists;
- public terms and privacy posture are written;
- backups and rollback are validated;
- no fake analytics or unverified impact claims are shown.

## Later

- Contributor applications.
- Author profiles.
- DOI/external identifier fields.
- Real analytics after privacy review.
- Exportable publication packs.

## Adopted public language

- **Primary promise:** Turn knowledge into traceable evidence.
- **Supporting idea:** preserve authorship, context and provenance around published work.
- **Organizational value:** make knowledge durable, reviewable and useful; build a research network rather than another document archive.
- **CTA vocabulary:** Explore Scholarium, See the workflow and Review the platform.

## Guardrails

The asset library contains broader B2B language about organizational decisions, sources and enterprise engagement. Scholarium must not claim a feature merely because it is an attractive marketing phrase. Until those capabilities exist and are verified, the public landing uses only the traceability, publication, authorship, provenance and open-source claims supported by the implementation.

The same anti-pay-to-rank rule applies to B2B messaging: no promise of bought reach, privileged discovery or status-based access.

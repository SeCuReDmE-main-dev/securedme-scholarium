# Scholarium product truth and route contract

## Public surfaces

| Surface | Route | Purpose | Public boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Landing | `/` | Explain the commons, provenance, Teach, and organization direction | Public pre-alpha only |
| Commons | `/app` | Signal, library, studio, saved work, migration, formalization, profiles | Identity and provider actions remain consent-gated |
| Teach | `/teach` | Student learning, strengths, portfolio, teacher evidence, administration | Synthetic learning data until external review |
| Public profile | `/profile/:publicId` | Owner-enabled public profile and public work | No private sections or drafts |
| Publication | `/publication/:publicationId` | Public artifact and provenance | Author-selected visibility only |
| Policy | `/privacy`, `/terms` | Current public policy surfaces | No claim of external certification |

## Preserved product contracts

- Publications, versions, relationships, interactions, discussion, repository links, media links, and provenance verification.
- Search, feeds, saved collections, profiles, author identifiers, notifications, ranking preferences, and local insights.
- Provider-consent and handoff surfaces for Google, GitHub, PayPal, ORCID, YouTube, and other prepared integrations.
- Teach lessons, progress, strengths, projects, goals, circles, recognitions, recaps, teacher interventions, organization dashboards, exceptional access, and consent records.
- Cohorts below ten people remain unavailable to organization comparisons.

## Invariants

1. Dignity and empowerment are product constraints, not marketing language.
2. Provenance records platform events and declared context; it does not certify truth.
3. Payment never changes reach, ranking, moderation, or publication rights.
4. Human review and scientific authority remain human.
5. Identity, provider connections, and public visibility require explicit consent.
6. No real minor data is admitted during public pre-alpha.

## Prohibited claims

- No production-readiness, school deployment, legal compliance, scientific authority, diagnostic capacity, or certification claim.
- No claim that a prepared provider handoff is a live connection.
- No generated metric, user count, adoption figure, or success rate.
- No public description of private HippoRAG, plithogenic, FfeD, or ranking mechanisms.

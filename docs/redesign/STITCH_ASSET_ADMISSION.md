# Stitch asset admission

## Classification

- `scholarium_soulful_dark_landing_full` and `scholarium_soulful_light_landing_full`: visual QA references only.
- `scholarium_cinematic_immersive_variant`: editorial composition reference only.
- `scholarium_tactile_archive_variant` and `scholarium_tactile_archive_mobile`: application density and responsive references only.
- Role-specific Teach boards: palette, hierarchy, and information-architecture references only.
- Existing canonical assets under `apps/web/public/brand`: approved web assets when crop, contrast, and text boundaries are respected.

## Rejections

- Full-screen captures are never embedded as interfaces.
- Bitmaps containing generated copy are not factual sources.
- Duplicate exports, mock metrics, fake activity, fake identities, and provider connection states are rejected.
- Identity geometry is not regenerated. The Education symbol may receive role-specific color treatment only.

## Retained asset contract

| Asset group | Role | Theme | Crop / safety |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `brand/logos/final/1.webp` | Scholarium mark | Both | Keep complete mark visible |
| `brand/campaigns/landing-hero-dark.webp` | Editorial hero media | Dark-first | Crop to visual subject; HTML supplies all copy |
| `brand/education/*` | Suite bridge and Teach identity | Paired dark/light | Preserve official geometry |
| `brand/icons/*` | Supporting visual vocabulary | Paired dark/light | Never substitute for interactive labels |

The same DOM is used for dark and light modes. Assets support the interface; they do not become the interface.

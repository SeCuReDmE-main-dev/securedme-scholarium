# Scholarium Teach privacy and EFVP gate

Status: pre-alpha design control. This document is an engineering preparation artifact, not legal advice or a completed EFVP.

## Processing inventory

| Record | Purpose | Default visibility | Retention trigger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Learning objective | Deliver a selected course | course-bound | course lifecycle |
| Learning attempt | Establish mastery evidence | private | review schedule plus account policy |
| Checkpoint | Resume exactly | private | replaced by newer checkpoint or deletion |
| Strength observation | Suggest a testable learning bridge | private, pending review | expiry, contest, or deletion |
| AlgoQuest event | Carry a bounded educational action | private | purpose completion |
| Learner assistant graph record | Resume learner-owned context | private owner only | expiry, replacement or deletion |
| Assistant exchange projection | Share one consent-bound pedagogical projection | sender and recipient only | seven-day expiry plus receipt policy |
| Weekly objective | Align support with the learner's school year | private | completion, deletion or school-year closure |
| Intervention preference | Control frequency, context and silence | private | replacement or account deletion |
| Growth story | Learner-controlled reflection | private | learner choice |
| Media request | Enforce quota and worker handoff | private | quota/audit window |
| Purpose consent | Prove grant or revocation | private | legal retention policy |

## Required controls

- Highest privacy settings by default.
- Separate consent for learning, personalization, profiling, sharing, and media.
- Guardian workflow for applicable minor accounts.
- No real student data before a completed EFVP and authorized pilot.
- Access, correction, export, withdrawal, and Teach-domain deletion.
- Clear separation of fact, observation, algorithmic hypothesis, human interpretation, and official decision.
- Human review for any consequential decision.
- No behavioral content in Datadog, FfeD proofs, plugins, or media receipts.
- Double-authorized, expiring, purpose-bound emergency access with an immutable audit receipt.

## EFVP completion evidence

The pilot gate requires a named privacy lead, approved data-flow map, legal basis by purpose, minor-consent analysis, retention schedule, processor inventory, cross-border assessment, incident procedure, deletion test, re-identification test, and signed residual-risk decision.

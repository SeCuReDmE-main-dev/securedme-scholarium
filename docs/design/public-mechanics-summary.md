# Public Mechanics Research Summary

This public note summarizes transformed product research from public Academia.edu pages and public support documentation. It does not include raw Firecrawl dumps or non-public content.

## Observed public mechanics

- Discovery centers on papers, topics/research interests, profiles, and keyword search.
- Upload flows revolve around file or no-file submission, title, description/abstract, authorship, and research-interest tagging.
- Research interests improve search visibility, feed placement, metadata signals, and topic browsing.
- Analytics/impact reporting exists as a product pattern, but SecuredMe Scholarium will not show fake metrics in pre-alpha.

## Scholarium translation

- Upload becomes `Apply to contribute` until alpha.
- Research interests become Omeka tags and collection routing.
- Papers/books/white papers become Omeka Items.
- Topic groups become Omeka Collections.
- Public profiles and account editing remain locked until governance and moderation are ready.

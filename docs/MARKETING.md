# Scholarium marketing guide

## Positioning

**Scholarium is the free social commons for serious work.** It gives research, teaching, projects, books, explainers, and open-source stories a place to carry their context, attribution, and next step forward.

## Approved short descriptions

- **One line:** A free social commons for research, learning, projects, and open knowledge.
- **Short:** Scholarium helps people publish work with context, learn from it in public, and stay discoverable without buying reach.
- **Long:** Scholarium is a SecuredMe Education pre-alpha platform for learners, teachers, researchers, maintainers, and independent builders. It combines readable discovery with provenance, non-blocking formalization guidance, and transparent feed preferences.

## Messages to lead with

1. **Discovery is not for sale.** Contributions never purchase reach, ranking, or publication rights.
2. **The work keeps its context.** A post can carry its sources, versions, files, license, questions, and attribution.
3. **QuaNthoR teaches form without policing voice.** It offers guidance; it never blocks publication for an imperfect draft.
4. **Open source has a social home.** Scholarium can tell a project’s story while collaboration and code changes remain on GitHub.
5. **Privacy has boundaries.** Optional personal activity insights stay local to the browser, and provider tokens are not stored as profile data.

## Audience language

| Audience | Lead with |
| --- | --- |
| Students | Turn a project into work people can understand, cite, and celebrate. |
| Teachers | Give student work a visible, contextual home without turning learning into a popularity contest. |
| Researchers | Share notes, protocols, reports, and questions with provenance and discovery controls. |
| Maintainers | Show the people and purpose behind an open project; route code collaboration to GitHub. |

## Proof and accuracy rules

Always say **pre-alpha** when describing the live service. Do not claim that Scholarium provides legal copyright registration, DOI/ISBN issuance, formal proof verification, clinical advice, paid reach, biometric storage, live payment checkout, or full public moderation operations.

Use “PayPal sign-in sandbox is configured” rather than “PayPal login is live.” Google and GitHub entry routes are implemented but require each provider’s own credential and review before launch.
